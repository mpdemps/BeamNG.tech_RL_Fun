# Ideas

This is **Mikey's idea capture file.** When Mikey has an idea — for a reward term, a new experiment, a track to try, a stunt to teach the agent, a question about why something works — it goes here. No idea is too small. No idea gets rejected at capture time; we just write it down with a date and a status, and decide later whether to try it.

We come back to this file when planning the next training run and when starting a new phase.

## Status legend

- **open** — captured, not yet tried.
- **tried** — attempted in a run; see `RUNS.md` for what happened.
- **discarded** — decided not to pursue (Mikey changed his mind, or we learned it doesn't apply). Keep the entry; don't delete history.
